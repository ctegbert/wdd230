Site Plan for Chamber Website

Site Name

The Chamber Hub

Selected because it reflects the central role of the chamber in the community, acting as a hub for businesses and organizations.

Site Purpose

  • Provide a platform for businesses to connect and network
  • Offer resources and support for local economic development

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #336699 (used for headings and accents)

Secondary Color: #CCCCCC (used for backgrounds)

Typography

Heading Font: Arial, sans-serif

Body Font: Roboto, sans-serif
